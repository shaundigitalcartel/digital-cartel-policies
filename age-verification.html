Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Age Verification Popup ("the App") is developed by Digital Cartel ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

The App collects no data whatsoever. It is a theme app extension that runs entirely within your Shopify theme.

  • No server-side storage — The App has no backend, no database, and no server.
  • No API calls — The App makes zero external requests.
  • Cookie (client-side only) — A single browser cookie is set on your visitor's device to remember that they have passed the age gate. This cookie contains no personal information — only a flag indicating verification status. The cookie duration is configured by you in the theme customizer.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Customer data (names, emails, addresses, payment info)
  • Date of birth or age information (entered values are checked client-side and immediately discarded)
  • Order or product data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Browsing behaviour of your store visitors
  • IP addresses

How the Age Gate Works

All verification logic runs in the visitor's browser:

  • The age gate overlay is rendered by Shopify's theme engine as part of your store's HTML
  • Verification responses (Yes/No click, date of birth entry, checkbox confirmation) are processed entirely in the browser using vanilla JavaScript
  • No data is sent to any server — ours or otherwise
  • A cookie is set locally to prevent repeat prompts for returning visitors

Data Storage & Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is nothing to store or secure on our end. The only stored item is the client-side cookie on the visitor's browser, which they can clear at any time.

Data Retention

There is no data to retain. When you uninstall the App:

  • The theme extension is removed by Shopify automatically
  • No data remains on any server because none was ever collected
  • Existing visitor cookies will expire naturally based on the duration you configured

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Zero external requests are made.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable or remove the age gate at any time via your theme customizer
  • Uninstall the App for complete removal
  • Contact us with any questions about this policy

GDPR & CCPA

Since the App collects no personal data from you or your store visitors, GDPR and CCPA obligations are minimal. The client-side cookie contains no personal information and is used solely for functionality (remembering verification status). We comply with both regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
