Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Custom CSS Editor ("the App") is developed by Digital Cartel ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

The App collects only what is necessary to function:

  • Shop domain — To associate your CSS with your store.
  • Session tokens — Standard Shopify OAuth tokens for authenticating your admin session.
  • CSS content — The custom CSS you write in the editor, stored so it persists between sessions.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Customer data (names, emails, addresses, payment info)
  • Order or product data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Browsing behaviour of your store visitors

The App requests only read_metaobjects and write_metaobjects scopes — the minimum required to store your CSS via Shopify's metafield system.

How We Use Your Data

Your CSS content is:

  • Stored in a database linked to your shop domain
  • Published to a Shopify app-owned metafield so your theme extension can display it
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never used for advertising or profiling

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on secure, encrypted infrastructure. Session tokens are managed by Shopify's official authentication library. We follow industry-standard security practices.

Data Retention

Your data is retained while the App is installed. When you uninstall the App:

  • Your session data is deleted automatically via Shopify's uninstall webhook
  • Your CSS content is removed from our database within 30 days
  • The theme extension and its metafield are removed by Shopify automatically

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data stays between you and Shopify.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your CSS at any time by clearing the editor and saving
  • Uninstall the App for complete data removal
  • Request a copy of your stored data by contacting us
  • Request immediate deletion of your data by contacting us

GDPR & CCPA

Since we do not collect personal data from your customers or store visitors, GDPR and CCPA obligations are minimal. We comply with both regulations. If you have questions about data processing, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
